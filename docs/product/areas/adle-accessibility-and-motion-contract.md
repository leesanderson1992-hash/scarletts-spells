# ADLE Accessibility And Motion Contract

## Purpose

This document owns the global accessibility, device, motion, and sound contract for 7-UI.

## Baseline Requirements

Every rich activity must support:

- touch;
- mouse;
- keyboard-only use;
- visible focus order;
- screen-reader labels for controls and state;
- reduced-motion mode;
- sound-off operation;
- text alternative for audio instructions;
- colour-independent status;
- minimum target sizes;
- non-drag alternatives through tap/select controls;
- low-powered device fallback.

## Motion And Sound

Motion is optional enhancement. Reduced-motion mode must preserve comprehension and completion.

Sound is optional enhancement. A child must not need sound to understand correctness or complete the assignment.

Independent recall should remain visually restrained where necessary to avoid answer leakage.

## Proof

Accessibility proof is recorded in the 7-UI proof register, then linked from affected matrix rows.
