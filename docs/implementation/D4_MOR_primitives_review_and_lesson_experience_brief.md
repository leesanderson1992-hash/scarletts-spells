# ADLE 7-UI-G — D4_MOR Interaction, Guide and Child-Experience Pilot

## Authority

This document is the repository authority for the gated `D4_MOR_PREFIXES_UN`
Word Lab pilot. It ratifies the combined 7-UI-G interaction brief and the
Synthesis-inspired experience addendum accepted on 2026-07-13. The immutable
D4_MOR v1 source remains linguistic source truth; pilot-only Guide copy,
sentences and prefix contrasts live in the separately versioned pilot data.

## Contract

The normal child route must deliver one continuous, responsive Word Lab:
discover `un-`, split and rebuild `unhappy`, classify NOT versus REVERSE,
choose `un-` from `un-`/`re-`/`pre-` before fixed `tidy`, complete four
look-cover-write-check attempts, complete four sentence dictations, and finish
through the existing lesson completion path. A child must be able to finish
without adult prompting or instructional text outside the Guide region.

Activation requires a valid version-1 assignment snapshot and both the private
server gate and child allowlist. Invalid, historical and ungated assignments
retain the current warm shell. The approved lesson words are `unfair`,
`unkind`, `unlock`, and `untidy`; `unhappy` is the anchor and `unnatural` plus
`unnecessary` are unassessed stretch words. The runtime never reads the source
package directly.

## Experience and interaction rules

- Words and morphemes are the teaching objects; action precedes explanation.
- One stable Word Lab scene, Guide presence, word stage and tray span the lesson.
- Guide beats are authored data with deterministic exploration, misconception,
  help and completion branches; recall beats are silent.
- Dragging always has touch, tap/select, keyboard and screen-reader parity.
- Lift, glow, attraction, resistance, fusion, cleave, shutter and pulse keep one
  meaning throughout the lesson.
- No red, failure language, points, streaks, punitive sound or answer exposure
  during independent recall.
- Motion uses shared 200ms snap, 300ms flip and 400ms pulse constants, with a
  meaningful reduced-motion alternative.
- `re- + tidy` truthfully yields `retidy`/“tidy again”; unsupported `pre- + tidy`
  does not fuse. Neither alternative is presented as the target.

## Payload, state and evidence

The root intro item stores one immutable `MorphologyLessonPayloadV1`; related
items store stable activity bindings. Guided manipulation is completion-only
and retains `guided_practice_attempt` with null correctness. Controlled spelling
and dictation retain the existing first-exposure evidence, scheduling and reward
semantics. Sentence dictation stores the full raw sentence in the attempt ledger
while a server-derived target token alone enters word-level completion logic.

Local resume stores meaningful activity state for seven days under assignment,
payload and content versions. It never stores pointer movement and never
reopens a covered answer. Cross-device resume is excluded.

## Acceptance

Acceptance requires automated payload/fallback/evidence regressions, pointer,
touch, keyboard, screen-reader and reduced-motion completion, one uninterrupted
real-route recording, database verification, and an observation ledger. The
gate stays disabled if validation, fallback, recall safety, idempotence,
accessibility or unchanged scheduler/reward behavior cannot be demonstrated.

## Exclusions

No general morphology selection, other morphology families, AI Guide, scoring
change, reward change, Word Treasure change, parent dashboard work, universal
resume platform or broad composer redesign is authorised.
